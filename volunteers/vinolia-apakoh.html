Vinolia Apakoh is the CEO and Founder of BlackCellence Network. She is a trailblazer, a passionate community champion who strongly believes in supporting and contributing to the Saskatchewan community. FAQ 's

Frequently Asked Questions

Where does my donation go?

Every year for our Gala, we partner with a beneficiary for whom the proceeds of our flagship event goes out to. We prioritize collaborating with other Non-profit Organizations with strong links to the community who engage in initiatives that enhance our community. This is a way to pour back in the place we hold so dear.

How does this help my community?

It is of benefit to the community because it keeps our community connected as we seek to improve it. As members of this community, we all have our separate journeys that differentiate us in little ways but also do have connecting points that bring us all together which come in our common needs of Self Actualization (Being the best versions of ourselves), Sociological (Feeling Safe and Valued) and of course, a strong sense of belonging.

How do you ensure transparency and accountability?

We ensure this by being accessible, anyone is welcome to write any concerns or suggestions in our suggestion box here (Include Hyperlink), we make it a point to also offer surveys for every event to gather opinions and ideas we can consider.

We ensure all suggestions are taken back to our drawing board for consideration, to be followed up on and for action to be taken when necessary.

Can I volunteer with your organization?

Volunteering with Blackcellence Network can be done through Expression of Interest. We always need volunteers for events we organize so keep an eye for our “Call for Volunteers” on our Social Media Platforms and website. Individuals interested in Volunteering can also reach us at info@blackcellencenetwork.com.
